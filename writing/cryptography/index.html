1.1 Introduction

Cryptography has existed for more than 4000 years, used by Egyptians[1]. It was used as a tool to protect national secrets and strategies.

In the modern day, the demand of cryptography started during 1960s when the private sector needed a way to protect their information digitally.

In 1977, D.E.S ( Data Encryption Standard ) was adopted for encrypting unclassified data.
